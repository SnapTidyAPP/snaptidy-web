Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026
Contact: snaptidyapp@outlook.com

Core Principle

SnapTidy is an on-device photo library tidy app. Photos, photo metadata, decisions, task progress, and Widget state are processed on your device and are not transmitted to SnapTidy servers.

Information Read on Device

  • Photo metadata such as creation date, file size, media type, and system photo identifiers, used to create daily sets and show progress.
  • Photo content loaded by iOS only for preview, swiping, offline preload, or Widget display.
  • Photo permission status, used to determine whether SnapTidy can show photos, write favorites, or start iOS delete confirmation.

Information Stored on Device

  • Decisions such as keep, review, and love.
  • Daily task progress, streaks, and completed counts.
  • Offline preparation state for travel or weak-network use.

Apple App Privacy Meaning

SnapTidy’s core data processing happens on device. Under Apple App Privacy terminology, data processed only on device and not transmitted in a form accessible to the developer is not collected by the developer. SnapTidy does not send photos, photo metadata, or decision records to our servers.

Delete Flow

Swiping left only marks a photo for review. A photo is moved to iOS Recently Deleted only after you confirm in SnapTidy and pass the iOS system delete confirmation. Recovery behavior is managed by Apple Photos.

Withdrawing Permission

You can change or revoke photo access anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos. If permission is revoked, SnapTidy cannot continue reading or tidying your photos. Uninstalling the app generally removes SnapTidy’s local app data.

Widget and App Group

Home and Lock Screen Widgets read local App Group data, such as the next thumbnail, task counts, and tidy status. This data is used only on your device and is not uploaded.

iCloud

If your photos are stored in iCloud, SnapTidy may use iOS system APIs to download photos or thumbnails from your own iCloud for display or offline preload. Apple handles that process. SnapTidy does not access your Apple ID or iCloud credentials.

Third Parties

The current version does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. If this changes, we will update this policy and App Store Connect privacy information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact snaptidyapp@outlook.com.
